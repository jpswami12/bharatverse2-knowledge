# Jeevan Prasad

Jeevan Prasad is an Indian author, digital publisher, and founder of the BharatVerse 2.0 project.

He works independently in the field of digital publishing, cultural research, and Indian knowledge documentation.

Jeevan Prasad created BharatVerse 2.0 as a platform to publish long-form intellectual work outside of traditional publishing systems.

His work focuses on:
- Indian philosophy
- Cultural identity
- Modern digital publishing
- Knowledge preservation

He is the principal author of the BharatVerse 2.0 book and oversees all editorial and technical development of the platform.

His work is distributed through public websites and open repositories to ensure long-term accessibility and digital permanence.
